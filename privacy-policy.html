Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2025

No Data Collection

My Vehicle Report does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All data remains exclusively on your device and is never accessed or shared with third parties.

1. Local Data Storage

All user data is stored locally on your iOS device:

  • Vehicle information (make, model, year, etc.)
  • Maintenance records and service history
  • Fuel tracking data and mileage information
  • Photos you add (receipts, odometer readings)

2. No External Sharing

The app does not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Transmit data to servers or cloud services
  • Use analytics, tracking, or advertising networks
  • Share data with third parties

3. Photos and Media

If you grant photo library access, the app only accesses photos you explicitly select. These are stored locally and never transmitted externally.

4. Data Control

  • All data remains on your device
  • You can export your data using built-in sharing features
  • Uninstalling the app removes all data from the device

5. Children’s Privacy

My Vehicle Report does not collect personal information from anyone under 13.

6. Apple Compliance

The app complies with Apple’s App Store guidelines. No analytics, tracking, or external data collection is performed. Users are fully informed and have complete control over their data.

7. Updates to This Policy

If the app’s data practices change, this Privacy Policy will be updated and communicated through app updates.
